CrystalsCollector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the round by matching your total score to the random number and you lose the round if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you each round until you click on it.

The game will change the value of each crystal at the start of each round.
